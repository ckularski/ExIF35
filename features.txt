ExIf 35 Features
February 14, 2016
----------------------

*Record vital information about film exposures
*Basic informatin that is found in exif
*Advanced film-only information stored
*Add tags/keywords to exposure records
*Store all exposures in files for their roll
*Access all rolls from a central library
*Write exposure information to scanned negatives/prints
*Embed copyright information in image files
*Backup/Restore settings with a build-in utility
*Import data from EXIF4FILM Android App
*Import data from Meta35 for Windows
